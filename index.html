About One Campus

A smarter path to your
first opportunity

One Campus is a centralized mobile platform designed for college and university students to discover and share campus events, connect with peers, and find local personal services — tutors, gym partners, study groups, and more. Our goal is to build a thriving student community hub that simplifies campus life and strengthens social and academic connections.

📅

Live Campus Calendar

Never miss an event. A live calendar shows what's happening on campus, organized around your interests.

🤝

Peer Connections

Find study partners, gym partners, or project collaborators who share your schedule and goals.

📋

Student Service Listings

Post or discover tutoring, gigs, and other student-run services all in one trusted place.

🔔

Personalized for You

Personalized notifications keep you connected to the clubs, events, and people that matter most to you.

The Problem

Campus life is scattered across too many places.

Students juggle emails, group chats, flyers, and a dozen different apps just to stay connected to what's happening around them. Finding a study partner, joining a club, or hearing about a campus event shouldn't be this hard.

One Campus changes that. We bring everything into one centralized hub — events, peer connections, student services, and local opportunities — so you can focus on your college experience, not hunting for information.
