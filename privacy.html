Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

One Campus ("we", "us", or "the app") is a campus social platform for college and high school students to connect through clubs, events, and campus life. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using One Campus, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided when you sign in with Google or create an account.
  • Profile information — details you choose to add, such as your major, campus, interests, and class schedule.
  • Content you create — posts, photos, comments, direct messages, clubs, and events you submit.
  • Connections — the friends you add and the clubs and events you join.
  • Location — if you turn it on, your approximate location is used to show your spot on the campus map and give walking directions. You can disable this at any time in your device settings or in the app.
  • Device & notification data — a push-notification token so we can deliver alerts you opt into, plus basic technical information needed to run the app.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and operate your account and profile.
  • To show you relevant clubs, events, friends, and campus activity.
  • To deliver direct messages and notifications you choose to receive.
  • To keep the community safe — reviewing reported content and enforcing our rules.
  • To maintain, improve, and troubleshoot the app.

3. How your information is shared

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only in these ways:

  • With other users — your name, profile details, posts, and (if you set one) your class availability are visible to other students so you can connect. Direct messages are visible only to you and the person you're messaging.
  • With service providers that power the app: Google Firebase (sign-in, database, hosting of your account data), Cloudinary (photo storage), and Expo (push-notification delivery). These providers process data only to provide their service to us.
  • For legal reasons — if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

4. Your choices & rights

  • Delete your account — you can permanently delete your account and associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account in the app. This removes your profile, posts, and account data.
  • Block & report — you can block other users and report posts or messages that break our rules.
  • Notifications — you control which notifications you receive in Settings, and can turn off location sharing whenever you like.
  • Access & corrections — you can view and edit your profile information in the app, or contact us using the details below.

5. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your profile, posts, and account data. Some information may remain in backups for a limited time before being overwritten.

6. Security

We use reputable providers (Google Firebase and Cloudinary) and reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No online service can be guaranteed 100% secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

7. Children's privacy

One Campus is intended for students 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, please contact us and we will remove it.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll change the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.
